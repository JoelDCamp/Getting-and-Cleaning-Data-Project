Codebook
Summary of means of selected variables from 2012 Samsung Galaxy S II accelerometer experiment

"1" "Subject"
	Number between 1 and 30 identifying subject who carried out experiment

"2" "Activity"
	Label indicating activity carried out by test subject
		1. Walking
		2. Walking Upstairs
		3. Walking Downstairs
		4. Sitting
		5. Standing
		6. Laying

"3" "1 tBodyAcc-mean()-X"
	Lists the mean of the average measurement of body linear acceleration along the X axis of movement

"4" "2 tBodyAcc-mean()-Y"
	Lists the mean of the average measurement of body linear acceleration along the Y axis of movement
